# offer-builder — What you sell

Produces `OFFER.md`: a sharp picture of the coach's main offer so the twin can write copy, handle objections, and build funnels that actually match what they sell. Reusable — coaches re-run this whenever the offer evolves.

## How to run it

One question at a time — voice note plus the same words as text, every time, so they can listen or read along. Probe vague answers hard — a fuzzy offer makes everything the twin drafts fuzzy. Plain language.

### The questions
1. **"What's your main offer — what do people actually buy?"**
2. **"What's the price?"**
3. **"Where's a client before they work with you, and where are they after? The transformation."**
4. **"How's it delivered, and over how long?"**
5. **"What makes you different from every other coach in your space?"**
6. **"Any guarantee, or how do you take the risk off them?"**
7. **"What's the objection you hear most before someone buys?"**

### Probing examples
- "Coaching." → "What's the actual promise? What do they walk away with?"
- "I'm just better." → "Better how — what specifically do you do that the others don't?"
- Transformation too soft: "They feel good." → "Before/after in concrete terms — what changes they can measure?"

## Write it
Draft `~/[TwinName]/OFFER.md` from `templates/OFFER.md`. Fill: the offer, price, before→after transformation, delivery + duration, differentiation, guarantee, top objection.

## Read it back
In the coach's cloned voice: **"Here's your offer the way I'd pitch it — sharpen anything that's not quite right."** Refine until it's tight.

## Hand off
"Offer locked. Now — who's this actually for?" → proceed to `icp-builder`.
