# rules-and-assembly — Set the rules, go live

The last step of setup. Two jobs: agree the guardrails so the twin is safe to trust, then assemble everything into the twin's brain and switch it on. After this, the coach has a fully working twin that knows their business.

## How to run it

One question at a time, by voice. Plain language — frame it as "the rules for how I work for you."

### The questions
1. **"What should I always do without being asked?"** (e.g. capture every idea, flag who's gone quiet, file things tidily)
2. **"What should I *never* do without checking with you first?"** — prompt the risky ones: spend money, message or DM clients, post publicly, delete things, send emails.
3. **"What am I free to just handle on my own?"** (the low-risk stuff they want off their plate)

## Assemble
1. Add the guardrails to `~/[TwinName]/SOUL.md` as a clear **Rules** section (Always / Never-without-asking / Free-to-handle). These are hard boundaries — honour them in every future session.
2. Confirm SOUL.md, BUSINESS.md, OFFER.md, and ICP.md all exist in `~/[TwinName]/`.
3. **Seed always-on memory:** write the durable, behaviour-shaping facts into the twin's built-in memory so they're loaded every session — the coach's name + how they talk, the business one-liner + revenue ballpark, the offer + price, the dream client in one line, and the never-without-asking rules. (Full docs stay on disk and are read on demand; memory holds only the always-true essentials.)

## Read it back
In the coach's cloned voice, summarise the rules: **"Here's how I'll operate — always do X, never do Y without asking, free to handle Z. Sound right?"** Adjust on feedback.

## Go live
Confirm: **"I'm set up. I know who you are, your business, your offer, and your dream client — and I sound like you. Ask me anything about your business, or tell me to build something."**

Suggest a first test: "Try me — ask me who your dream client is, or tell me to draft a post."

## Notes
- The four docs are the twin's brain on disk; memory is the always-loaded summary. Keep memory tight — essentials only.
- The Never-without-asking list maps to keeping Hermes' approval prompts ON for risky actions. Don't let the twin bypass them.
