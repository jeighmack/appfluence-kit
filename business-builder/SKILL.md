# business-builder — Your business at a glance

Produces `BUSINESS.md`: the top-level picture of how the coach makes money, where their time goes, and what they run on. This is the parent doc — its key facts get seeded into the twin's always-on memory so the twin never forgets the basics.

## How to run it

One question at a time — voice note plus the same words as text, every time, so they can listen or read along. Probe thin answers. Plain language — no "operations", no "tech stack", just "how you make money" and "what tools you use."

### The questions
1. **"What's the business called, and how do you make money right now?"**
2. **"Roughly what are you doing a month — ballpark, no judgment?"**
3. **"Walk me through a typical week. Where does the time actually go?"**
4. **"What's the one thing eating most of your time that you'd kill to hand off?"**
5. **"Anyone on your team, or is it all you?"**
6. **"What tools and platforms are you running on?"** — capture every one (Stripe, Trainerize/Everfit, Hapana, Kajabi, Calendly, Gmail, Instagram, Sheets, etc.). This list is how every draft speaks their language — "log it in Hapana", "send it from Everfit" — without the twin ever wiring into anything.
7. **"Where do you want this in 12 months?"**

### Probing examples
- "I coach online." → "One-to-one? Group? Course? What's the split?"
- "The usual tools." → "Name them — where do payments land, where do clients live, where do you book calls?"

## Write it
Draft `~/[TwinName]/BUSINESS.md` from `templates/BUSINESS.md`. Fill: what the business is, revenue ballpark, weekly time map, biggest time-drain, team, tools/platforms list, 12-month goal.

## Read it back
In the coach's cloned voice: **"Here's your business as I understand it — what did I miss?"** Refine until accurate.

## Hand off
"Got the big picture. Now let's nail exactly what you sell." → proceed to `offer-builder`.
