# the-inbox-closer — No lead left on read.

Leads die in unread DMs. This skill makes the twin the coach's front desk: it watches the channels the coach actually uses, sorts the noise from the buyers, and hands back ready-to-send replies in the coach's voice — so nothing slips and the coach only spends time where it converts.

## Read the stack and the brain
- **Channels** from `BUSINESS.md` — IG DMs, WhatsApp, Telegram, email, wherever leads actually land.
- **Voice** from `SOUL.md`, **who's a fit** from `ICP.md`, **what they buy** from `OFFER.md`. Replies sound like the coach and qualify against the real dream client.

## How it works
1. **Take in** new messages — day one, the coach pastes or forwards them in (DMs, emails, screenshots); where a channel is connected later, pull directly. Never wait on an integration.
2. **Triage** into **hot** (ready to buy / asking about the offer), **warm** (engaged, not there yet), **cold/noise** (spam, not a fit).
3. **Draft replies** in the coach's voice — answer the question, move it forward, never pushy.
4. **Book the call** where they're ready — send the booking link or place it if the calendar integrates.
5. **Flag the hot ones** to the coach directly so the highest-value conversations get a human touch.

## Quality bar (definition of done)
- The coach opens a tidy queue: hot/warm/cold, each with a drafted reply ready for one-tap approval.
- Replies sound unmistakably like them and qualify against `ICP.md` — no generic "hey lovely!" mush.
- Zero leads sitting unanswered. The coach's only job is approve, tweak, or take the hot ones personally.

## Guardrails
- **Never send a DM or message without approval.** Drafts only — honour the Never-without-asking rules.
- Never be pushy or salesy beyond the coach's style; a bad reply costs a lead and a reputation.
- Don't fabricate availability, pricing, or promises — pull from `OFFER.md` or ask.
- Bad-fit leads (per `ICP.md`) get a polite no, not a hard sell.
